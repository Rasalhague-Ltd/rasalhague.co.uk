Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This policy explains how Rasalhague Ltd handles personal data collected through this website. At this stage the only personal data we collect is the email address you choose to give us.

Rasalhague Ltd (Companies House No. 17186277) is the data controller. Registered office: 9 St Georges Place, Brighton, BN1 4GB. You can contact us at hello@rasalhague.co.uk.

What we collect

If you submit the sign-up form, we collect your email address. We do not ask for any other personal information.

We use your email address solely to contact you about Rasalhague — primarily to let you know when the platform opens, and occasionally with related updates. Our lawful basis is your consent, given when you submit the form. You can withdraw that consent at any time.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data and we do not share it for marketing. Your email may be processed by the third-party tools we use to operate the site and send email; these providers act on our instructions and only for the purposes above.

How long we keep it

We keep your email until launch and for a reasonable period afterwards, or until you ask us to remove it — whichever comes first.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to have it corrected or erased, to object to or restrict its use, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email hello@rasalhague.co.uk.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been handled improperly.

Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us at any time to be removed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product develops. The current version will always be available on this page.
